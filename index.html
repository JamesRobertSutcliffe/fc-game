Panagram Land

Unscramble the panagram by clicking on each letter to spell out the word. You have 30 seconds to find the correct solution. If you find the correct word you will level up with a new panagram revealed with an extra letter available. If you manage to complete level 9 with a 9 letter solution you will complete the game. Log your name and level attained in the high scores list.
